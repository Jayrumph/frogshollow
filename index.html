About This Space

Cosy 1-bedroom self-contained cabin on a 13-acre lifestyle block in Morison Bush with sweeping South Wairarapa views. Sleeps 2, plus a fold-out couch for up to 4 (compact but comfortable, with storage shed for luggage). Enjoy sunsets, stargazing, and a dam surrounded by 100's of native trees. Just 10 mins to Greytown, 12 to Martinborough, with fishing and swimming at Morison Bush camping grounds nearby. Hosts live on-site and are happy to help with any requests. Parking for camper van or caravan.
